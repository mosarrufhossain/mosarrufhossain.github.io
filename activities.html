Club Activities

MIST Aeronautics & Astronautics Club

President and Co-Founder (January 2017 – July 2017)

MIST Aeronautics & Astronautics Club is a student-based organization functioning with around 150 students of the Aeronautical Engineering department with a mission of showing technical ability in the field of aeronautics & astronautics at various international and national competitions.

  • Drafted the constitution and organogram of the organization as a founding member, later accepted by the Head of the Aeronautical Engineering department.
  • Led a student committee consisting of 15 members toward planning and organizing member recruitment and the inauguration ceremony.

MIST Robotics Club

Vice President (July 2016 – July 2017)

MIST Robotics Club is a student-based organization working with a vision of engaging youth in the field of robotics.

  • Designed a member recruitment strategy that increased member recruitment by 50%.
  • Organized an inter-university robotics competition named ‘Robolution’ in 2016 and 2017.
  • Managed a team of 100 volunteers at Robolution’17 as Head Volunteer Coordinator.
  • Launched a yearly robotics-based magazine named ‘Singularity 2.0’ as an editor.
Community Based Projects

STEM for Super Girls

Project Coordinator (December 2019 – Present)

Planned the operation of the whole project to promote STEM education to girl students in Bangladesh through STEM workshops (Think STEM), career-oriented webinars (STEM Talk), and STEM-based competitions (STEM Fiesta). Prepared the project report and pitched the idea in the EMK Center Small Grant competition 2020. This project is the recipient of the EMK Center Small Grant competition 2020.

Around 1000 students actively participated in 7 different events and 6 competitions from all over Bangladesh. A small network was created between young STEM enthusiast girls and established women in STEM fields to bridge the information gap about careers.

School of Skills

Project Coordinator (July 2019 – December 2019)

Planned, designed and executed a community-based capstone project along with students to promote productive lifestyle practices through education on essential skills like time management, resource management, financial management, and goal-oriented strategic planning.
