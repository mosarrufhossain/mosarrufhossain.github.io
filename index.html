A mission-driven aerospace researcher who thrives in building systems, mentoring people, and advancing UAS technology for real-world environmental impact.

Welcome! I am Mosarruf Hossain. A driven Ph.D. student of Aerospace Engineering specializing in the application of sensing, estimation, and control with Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) at the University of Kansas. With a Master's degree in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Kansas, my expertise spans wind/gust/wake sensing and estimation, system identification of UAS, UAS system and flight control design, and control engineering.

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me at hossainmosarruf (at) gmail (dot) com.
